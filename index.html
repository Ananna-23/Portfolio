Intro

I am Sadia Tasnim Ananna, a student of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Asia Pacific, enrolled in the Spring 2023 batch.

My decision to pursue CSE was inspired by the guidance and encouragement of my elder brother. While I have yet to achieve any milestones in this field, I am committed to learning and continuously striving to improve my skills and stay aligned with the evolving demands of the discipline.

Skills

I have acquired foundational knowledge in several programming languages, including C, C++, Java, Python, SQL, HTML, and CSS. My understanding of these technologies allows me to approach problem-solving with a logical and structured mindset. As a student of Computer Science and Engineering, I am deeply interested in how technology shapes the modern world, and I am eager to explore these areas in greater dpeth.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I am also an artist with a passion for digital illustration. Creativity plays a significant role in both my technical and artistic interests, and I enjoy finding ways to blend the two whenever possible.

work

I am a freelance digital artist with experience in working with many international clients. My artistic style is primarily influenced by Japanese animation, commonly known as Anime, which has significantly shaped my creative direction. I initially began offering commissions through Fiverr and have since transitioned to VGen, a widely recognized platform for professional digital artists.

Creating art has always been a deep passion of mine. I find joy in bringing characters and ideas to life through illustration, and I continuously strive to refine my skills and explore new techniques. I am committed to growing as an artist while sharing my work with a global audience.
